# Data Product Configuration

Configure a complete data product with databases, roles, warehouses, and cost controls

This repeatable workflow guides you through configuring a complete data product in Snowflake. You'll plan the data product structure, create databases and schemas organized by zones, establish role-based access control with owner, writer, and reader roles, provision compute warehouses sized for your workload, and configure resource monitors for cost management. Run this workflow once for each data product you need to deploy.
